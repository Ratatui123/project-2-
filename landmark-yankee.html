Yankee Stadium

Home of the New York Yankees and New York City FC, an iconic sports venue in the Bronx.

Experience the history, excitement, and architectural homage of this legendary ballpark. A must-see landmark for sports fans and visitors alike.

Featured Landmark

Yankee Stadium in the Bronx — home of legendary baseball moments and modern sporting events.

Originally opened in April 1923, the stadium became a centerpiece of American sports history, hosting World Series games and iconic moments. Nicknamed “The House That Ruth Built,” it was closely associated with baseball legend Babe Ruth.

The original stadium was replaced in 2009 by a modern stadium that preserves many elements of the historic ballpark’s exterior and field while providing updated amenities for visitors and sports fans.
